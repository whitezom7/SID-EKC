Get involved

How to get involved

Take to media!

media outlets can truly help multiply the global reach of Safer Internet Day by using this occasion as an opportunity to explore the importance of online safety and digital literacy for children and young people in your media production

Most importantly!

Keep your self safe online! If you keep yourself online, raise awareness, and help others, you will make a lot of difference to many peoples lives.

Be the change

You can be the change you want to see in the (online) world by being kind and respectful to others online, by protecting your online reputation and that of others, and by seeking out positive opportunities to create, engage and share online.
